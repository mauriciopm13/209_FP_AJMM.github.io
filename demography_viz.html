Explore demographic trends

The goal of this visualization is to aid on the task of exploring patterns and discovering trends of average daily macronutrient consumption in United States across gender, race and age. You can select the nutrient you are interested in using the scrollbar at the top of the graphs. The color bar at the bottom of the heatmap serves as an ordering scale: the darker the color of the square, the higher the average consumption of the selected nutrient for a given combination of gender and age will be, although you can hover over the square to see the exact value. You can select/deselect a specific gender, race or a combination of both by clicking/double-clicking on the text or the squares on the heatmap to see a granularized version of the average consumption per age.
